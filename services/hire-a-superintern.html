Hire a Superintern

We know that hiring, training and getting work out of new interns can often be a pain that does not provide the returns for it, but we’re here to change that for the better.

‍No more scouting, training, or FOBG (fear of being ghosted) by a new intern.

If you’re a web3 founder looking for talented young folks to help you build your web3 project, this is for you.

The SuperteamDAO’s SuperIntern Program connects you with the brightest and the most talented young people in Web3. We know that hiring, training and getting work out of new interns can often be a pain that does not provide the returns for it, but we’re here to change that for the better.

The SuperIntern program is designed in a way that you will gain access to the best talent (we’ll handle the filtering for you), incentivised to help you and a structure enabling you to maximise the value you get out of your intern. You’ll also gain access to a community of problem solvers who get shit done one way or another who could become the people you hire full-time in the future.

At a Glance

  • Pre-filtered intern candidates from the Superteam community.
  • Required stipend of $500/month for the interns (we don’t take a penny).
  • Define the KPIs and deliverables required of the interns at the beginning of the internship.
  • Follow our suggested program structure or create your own to ensure the deliverables are met.
