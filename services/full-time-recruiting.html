🎁 The Process

Once you let us know about your recruiting needs, we can start sending candidates over within 48 hours. Here’s how the process works:

Step #1: Align

Together, we’ll identify your hiring criteria so that we can effectively filter the applicants to your team's needs and culture.

Step #2: Discover

We’ll receive and vet applications and send you a portal that includes the profiles of the best  candidates.

Step #3: Hire

Take 30 days to evaluate the candidate as a full time team member. Only pay the recruiting fee if they are a clear long-term fit.

🤝 Working with Superteam Talent

We’re excited to work with any fast-growing Web3 projects, with a few requirements:

Public Salary

To ensure effective matching and expectation setting, we require that the position have a stated and public salary range.

Public Fundraise

To ensure talent can feel confident about the role, we require that our partners have a public fundraising announcement of at least a pre-seed round.

Fully Remote

The future of work is global, and the members of our Talent Network are explicitly looking for remote-only work. If your role isn’t remote-friendly, we won’t be able to help.

❓ FAQs

  1. How much should I pay for (XYZ) position?
    Web3 salaries are rapidly changing, so we created this helpful guide to what we see as market standards here: 💰 Web3 Salary Reference Guide
  2. Do I have to pay just to list a position with Superteam?
    No you don't! We'll only ask for a fee if/when your position is filled with someone we've referred.
  3. I don’t have a job description yet - do you have templates we can use?
    Yup! Just check out the link below 🪴 Recruiting with Superteam
  4. How do the fees work?
    There is a recruiting fee of 15% of first year’s salary, due only if we successfully find a match. No match, no fees. The fees are due upon the offer letter being signed, even if there is a waiting period before the talent joins your team.
  5. How long does the process take?
    It depends on how niche your position is. Product Designers and Front End Developers may take as little as 2 weeks, while Senior Smart Contract Engineers can take longer.
